About me

Frontend Developer | Specializing in Beautiful, Accessible, and Efficient Web Interfaces.

As a frontend developer, I am driven by the belief that the best web interfaces are those that are beautiful, accessible, and highly efficient. Since beginning my coding journey, I've fallen in love with crafting user experiences that feel intuitive and look stunning.

While new to the professional industry, I bring a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My approach to design is rooted in the principles of usability, backed by the extensive knowledge in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) I've gained throughout the years in the Informatics programme at UNISA. I ensure modern user experiences by often incorporating responsive frameworks like Bootstrap.
